Smart Access and Attendance Solutions for Secure, Efficient Management.

ROBOFLAIR Access Control system enables you to oversee your facility by monitoring and setting up controlled access points at primary entrances, secure rooms, elevators, stairwells, and doors. This is particularly important for organizations that need to track employee attendance, as time and attendance systems facilitate the management, monitoring, and reporting of employee attendance.

Moreover, keyless entry has demonstrated its effectiveness in managing employee safety and turnover while safeguarding a company's infrastructure against both internal and external losses. As a component of a business security alarm system, access control can serve as a significant cost-saving measure by removing the need for re-keying with every employee exit.
